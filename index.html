This first visualization displays a map featuring countries within continents that have the total number of active Covid cases.

Discover the interactive Scatter plot, providing in-depth COVID-19 insights. As we hover over data points, detailed information about active cases, total deaths, total cases, and recoveries is revealed. The legend on the right uses colors to identify continents, making it easy to spot countries. Additionally, point size indicates population count. To enhance the exploration, I've included a filter at the end of the visualization, allowing to focus on specific continents.
